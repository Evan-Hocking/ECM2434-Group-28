Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our university project Carbon Foodprint. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that any personal information that you 
provide to us is treated with the utmost care and respect. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

The only information that we collect is your email address, which is used for authentication of your account and possibly used for notifications. We do not
collect any other personal information from you.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely for the purpose of authentication of your account and to possibly send you notifications related to your account or the
project. We will never share your email address with any third parties.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the protection of your information very seriously. The passwords of our users are encrypted in our database, so your password is always kept secure.
We also take steps to ensure the security of our servers and the data stored on them.

Data Retention

Currently, users cannot delete their profiles, so no personal data is being deleted. However, we will delete your email address and any associated data
upon your request.

Third-Party Services

We uses third-party services called OpenFoodFacts that only collects item informations. No third-party services are collecting personal user-information.

Legal Requirements

We are committed to complying with all applicable laws and regulations related to privacy and data protection.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact Evan at evan@hotmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please check back periodically to stay
up-to-date.

Thank you for using our Carbon Foodprint project!
